Workshop Structure — 4 Acts, ~2 Hours

Click the section you'd like to explore visually first

1

Wie KI funktioniert (~30 min)

Deep dive: Tokenisierung → Embeddings → Attention → Neuronales Netz → Next-Token-Prediction → Training. The technical foundation that makes everything else click.

2

Grenzen & Halluzinationen (~15 min)

Why AI lies convincingly, what we know vs. don't know, patent-specific risks. The "aha moment" where technical understanding meets practical consequences.

3

Prompting-Techniken (~30 min)

Zero-Shot, Few-Shot, Chain-of-Thought, Role Prompting, der Prompt-Baukasten (Rolle + Kontext + Aufgabe + Format). Side-by-side neural network comparison animation.

4

Praxis-Übungen (~30 min)

3 hands-on exercises: Prompt verbessern, Halluzination erkennen, Patent-Prompts bauen. Group work with discussion.
